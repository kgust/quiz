Gustavson Post-Christmas Quiz 2008

This is the 19th annual Gustavson Post-Christmas Quiz! We welcome all quiz newcomers to our traditional holiday greeting. The longtime recipients love to test their wit and wisdom every year (except last year when creating the Quiz never made it to the top of our ‘things to do’ list). You can tally your answers to the multiple-choice questions at the end. On your mark, get set, go!


  1. Why have we been especially praising the Lord in recent years?
    1. Kevin accepted a new full-time position in June 2007 as a senior web developer at Signature Tech Studios, a small business in Geneva – 10 minutes from our house.
    2. After Thanksgiving 2007, we felt His protection and provisions following our high-speed slide into the ditch on icy I-94 west of Fargo, ND on the way home from the Gustavson family farm. We suffered no injuries, and the van sustained minor damage from the accident. We enjoyed a three-day visit with our friend and Fargo resident, Teresa Nygard, who showed tremendous hospitality to us while our vehicle was being repaired.
    3. Rose is nearly recuperated from surgery on Nov. 18, 2008 to remove a benign tumor from her left pelvic bone. Our church family, relatives, and friends sustained us for six weeks with childcare, meals, school rides for Abby, house cleaning, and washing laundry.
